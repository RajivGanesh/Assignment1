There are many problems that which people in the agricultural sector are impaired by. Sometimes these obstacles are unforeseen, in which proactive solutions were not planned. This presents farmers, and produce retailers with a difficult business model that requires them to take a reactive approach to annual problems. This web application attempts to establish the agricultural sector directly to an existing market, overstepping commercial means of acquiring natural produce. It aims to make raw produce accessible to local industries, such as restaurants, and food processing industries. This helps to mitigate the problem of food waste in times of social, political, and economic uncertainty. It would also help reduce the economic backlash of an increasing food import bill, by applying a new platform to derive new logistics with regard to the movement of fresh produce within a country.
